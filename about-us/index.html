About Us

Empowering the next generation of healthcare professionals through excellence in education, innovation, and leadership.

About Apollo Knowledge

Apollo Knowledge is a pioneering initiative by the Apollo Hospitals Group, dedicated to transforming healthcare education in India and beyond. Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic excellence and real-world healthcare needs, equipping students with the skills, knowledge, and values required to excel in allied health sciences. Backed by decades of healthcare leadership, Apollo Knowledge integrates advanced learning methodologies, global best practices, and strong industry linkages to nurture the healthcare leaders of tomorrow.

About Apollo Allied Health Academy

Apollo Allied Health Centres are state-of-the-art teaching campuses established across India to deliver industry-aligned B.Sc programs in fields like Medical Lab Technology, Radiology, Critical Care, and Operation Theatre Technology.
These Centres are built around three core principles:

  • Clinical Immersion: Every student is embedded into the Apollo hospital ecosystem with structured internships and exposure to real cases.
  • Academic Excellence: Programs follow the latest UGC and healthcare sector guidelines, with simulation labs and experienced faculty.
  • Career Outcomes: Our curriculum is designed to meet employer demand. Students graduate with real-world skills that lead to jobs — not just degrees.

Our Mission

  • Undertake and promote translational and clinical research in major medical disciplines, focusing on translating research into practical applications.
  • Bridge the gap between classroom learning and clinical reality.
  • Offer students immersive training in actual healthcare settings.
  • Build a high-quality, job-ready allied health workforce for India and the world.

Our Vision

To become India’s most trusted healthcare education network by setting new benchmarks in training allied health professionals who are skilled, ethical, and globally competent.

Our Strengths

  • Exclusive not-for-profit, non-governmental research foundation for the largest healthcare chain Apollo Hospitals Group
  • DSIR recognition for over 14 years
  • Supported by NABH-certified Institutional Ethics Committees
  • High-quality scientific/technical expertise in research designs
  • Centralized and shared resources to carry out research
  • In-depth research expertise knowledge in molecular biology

Our Leadership

Mr V. Sivaramakrishnan

Chief Executive Officer, Apollo Knowledge

Sivaramakrishnan is renowned for turning businesses around with new initiatives bringing on board 30+ years of global experience in various sectors such as FMCG, Automobiles, e-Commerce and Education across UK, Dubai, Malaysia and India. Dr. H Vinod Bhat

Vice Chancellor, The Apollo University

Prof. H. Vinod Bhat took charge as the Vice Chancellor of The Apollo University on 1.7.2022. He has been a medical doctor, teacher, researcher and administrator for the past 34 years. As the Vice Chancellor of the Manipal Academy of Higher Education, he was responsible for the University being conferred the coveted tag of Institution of Eminence, which was one of only three such conferments in the Private sector. He has been an institution builder par excellence, setting up two campuses, five University departments and over 100 University centres. He has very strong international University networks, having been the Co-Chair for the FICCI Higher Education Committee in the year 2017 – 18.

Dr. Jagadeswaran

Dean & Head, Allied & Healthcare Professions

Dr. Jagadeswaran, a pioneer in Allied Health Professions, has over 22 years of experience in academia, clinical practice, research, and administration. He is the first in India to earn a Doctorate in Renal Sciences and Dialysis Technology. He has held leadership roles at institutions like Sri Ramachandra University and Saveetha University, and worked in the corporate sector with Baxter. A key figure in national policy, he currently serves as Lead Coordinator of the Provisional Regulations Committee and Council Member of the National Commission for Allied and Healthcare Professions under the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare. He is also the former Chairperson of its Curriculum Committee.
