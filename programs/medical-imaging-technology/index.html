B.Sc Medical Imaging Technology

Empowering Diagnostic Imaging Experts.
A robust 3-year degree program that trains students in advanced medical imaging and diagnostic techniques.

Know More Apply Now

Program Highlights

Modern Curriculum

Integrated theory and practical training in radiography, CT, MRI, and ultrasound techniques.

Clinical Practice

Live imaging sessions and hospital-based internships across Apollo Hospitals and affiliates.

NAAC A++ Accreditation

Globally recognized degree backed by the highest accreditation for educational standards.

Advanced Imaging Labs

Hands-on training with state-of-the-art digital X-ray, MRI, CT scanners, and PACS systems.

Course Structure

Year Key Subjects
1 Anatomy & Physiology, Basic Physics, Radiographic Techniques I, Communication Skills, Medical Ethics, Computer Applications, Clinical Orientation
2 Radiographic Techniques II, Digital Imaging, Contrast Radiography, Radiation Safety, CT Imaging Basics, Pathology, Clinical Rotations
3 MRI Techniques, Advanced CT Imaging, Ultrasound Imaging, PACS Management, Quality Control, Research Methodology, Internship & Project Work
